Updates to our Privacy Policy
Hi there,
At Blinkist, we continually strive to give you clear information about - and control over - your personal data in connection with your Blinkist account and your visit to our website. This is why we’ve made some changes to our Privacy Policy to bring it up to date.
As Blinkist grows and expands into new regions, these updates were necessary to reflect some international legislative developments. Furthermore, we would like to keep you properly informed about our closer cooperation with Go1 (the company that acquired Blinkist a few months ago - please see our blog post and FAQ), which may require the joint processing of data from time to time. As our companies are increasingly growing together, we are leveraging the many opportunities to improve processes across both companies including administrative functions. Our joint management of privacy related matters is a great example of how we are developing products and common processes, with privacy in mind, to support all of the companies within the Go1 Group, and their users - like you!
If you have any questions about this update, we will be happy to help you. Please note that notwithstanding the acquisition by Go1, your account remains with Blinkist, and we will continue to be your contact for all inquiries. The easiest way to reach us is at support@blinkist.com . Besides that, there is no need for you to take any action - this update is for your information only.
Kind regards,
Your Blinkist Team
